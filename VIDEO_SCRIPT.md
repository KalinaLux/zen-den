## Production Notes

### To make this video:

**Option A — Fastest (AI avatar, ~10 min)**
1. Go to [HeyGen.com](https://heygen.com) or [Synthesia.io](https://synthesia.io)
2. Paste the voiceover script
3. Upload screenshots of each screen as backgrounds
4. Pick an avatar and voice → Generate

**Option B — Screen recording + AI voice (~30 min)**
1. Record your screen walking through the app (QuickTime → New Screen Recording)
2. Paste the voiceover text into [ElevenLabs.io](https://elevenlabs.io) to generate the AI voice
3. Combine in iMovie or CapCut (both free)

**Option C — Fully polished (~1 hr)**
1. Screen record each scene separately
2. Generate voice with ElevenLabs (use "Rachel" or "Adam" voice)
3. Edit in CapCut or DaVinci Resolve (free)
4. Add lo-fi background music from Uppbeat.io (free, royalty-free)
5. Add text overlays for key features
